Welcome

I'm a web developer and expert in Geographic Information Systems (GIS) with experience in ruby, rails, javascript, html, css, jquery, test driven development (rspec, capybara, jasmine), web mapping, ArcGIS, ArcMap, remote sensing and image analysis. I'm seeking short- and long-term opportunities that make a difference in people's lives, and where I can continue to learn and grow as a developer, scientist, and human person.

Three recent projects I am excited about:

  • Rebioma Maps (codesite: https://github.com/tomay/rebmaps3, live: http://maps.rebioma.net) Recent refactoring (with @raorao) shows how much I have recently learned.
  • Makeupedia (codesite: https://github.com/tomay/makeupedia, live: http://makeupedia.herokuapp.com/) Allows anyone to edit a wikipedia page to amaze their friends and alter reality. Responsive design (using jQuery Mobile); authenticated users can save and share edits to wiki pages.
  • Ruby Maxent API (codesite: https://github.com/tomay/model-manager, in use: http://data.rebioma.net) This Ruby library to generate species distribution models from a database of species occurrences and taxonomic names.
